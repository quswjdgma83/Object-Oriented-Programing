text: "My! Look behind you, aunt!"
The old lady whirled round, and
snatched her skirts out of danger;
the lad fled, on the instant,
scrambled up the high board-fence,
and disappeared over it.
His aunt Polly stood surprised a moment, and then broke
into a gentle laugh.
